Sign in to your CRA account

Information alerts

Backup multi-factor authentication being introduced

When signing in to your CRA account, you will be prompted to add a backup multi-factor authentication option if you do not already have one. Users will have the option to skip this step during tax filing season.

Sweet (GCKey service) class action:

On March 31, 2026, the Federal Court will hold a hearing to decide whether it will approve a proposed settlement agreement in the Sweet Class Action. Learn more about the proposed settlement.

Email notification-Sign in to your CRA account

You may have recently received an email notification from the CRA. We are encouraging you to sign in to your CRA account to ensure you have online access prior to the tax season. If you have been locked out because you forgot your sign-in information, you may be able to regain access to your CRA account.

Sign in or register to access My Account, My Business Account, Represent a Client, Registered Plan Administrator Account, and Non-Resident Withholding Tax.

    Signing in on behalf of someone else

    If you are signing in on behalf of someone else (including friends and family members) you must use Represent a Client in your CRA account to access their information.

    Do not use autofill on a shared device

    Do not use autofill for your online banking information if you are on a shared device. When you are on your Sign-In Partner's website, ensure it is your information that is entered and not that of somebody else.

    If you register with someone else's banking credentials by mistake and link your SIN to them, the other person will have access to your tax information.

    If you have forgotten your CRA password:

      If you can’t access your account

      If you lost access to your CRA account, going through the registration process again may help you get your access back.

      More help with CRA user ID and password

      You did not receive your one-time passcode

      Try the following options to receive your one-time passcode:

      • Ask for the passcode to be sent again, or sent to a different phone number that you have already enrolled with
      • Use the Call me option instead of Text me if you are using a VoIP service
      • Use the passcode grid if you are already enrolled with that option
      • Use the third-party authenticator app if it is already set up

      If none of these options are available to you or you still can't receive your one-time passcode, you will need to contact us.

      More help with multi-factor authentication

      You have not received your CRA security code

      If your CRA security code has been lost or has not arrived after 10 business days, you can either:

      • Use the document verification service instead to verify your identity
      • Contact us to get a new one by mail

      If your CRA security code has expired, you can either:

      • Use the document verification service instead to verify your identity
      • Sign in to request a new CRA security code to be sent by mail
      Update your information with the CRA

      Inform the CRA about changes to your direct deposit or contact information, such as address, phone number or name changes.

      Update your information with the CRA
      Your account is locked
      Your CRA user ID and password have been revoked

      If your CRA user ID and password have been revoked, you can regain access to your account in either of the following ways:

      • Use a different sign-in option
      • Go through the registration process again to get a new CRA user ID and password

      More help with revoked CRA user ID and password

      Change the option you sign in with

      If you registered with a provincial partner, you can also create a CRA user ID and password or use a Sign-In Partner.

      If you registered with a CRA user ID and password, you can sign in with your CRA user ID and password and revoke your credential so you can register with a different sign-in option or create a new CRA user ID and password.

      If you registered with a Sign-In Partner, you can sign in to your current Sign-In Partner and switch to a different Sign-In Partner.
